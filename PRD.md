# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

The traditional PDF resume is too static to fully demonstrate the technical depth and creative impact of a Business Analytics candidate. Specifically, it fails to show the process and tools behind significant outcomes, such as the 15–20% efficiency gains achieved during the DHL Supply Chain internship or the strategic branding for Iowa Andhi.

This landing page solves that by providing a dynamic, curated professional story that combines technical evidence (data projects) with creative leadership (social media) to prove value to recruiters in a way a document cannot.

---

## 02 — Target Audience

**Primary:** Early-career recruiters and hiring managers at mid-to-large finance, consulting, and logistics firms hiring for Business Analyst, Data Analyst, or Operations Analyst roles.

**Secondary:** University of Iowa alumni and professional networking contacts discovered via LinkedIn.

**User Needs:**

* Quick, scannable understanding of who I am
* Validation of technical skills (Python, SQL, Power BI)
* Evidence of real-world business impact
* Clear graduation timeline (May 2026)
* Easy access to contact and professional links
